About Us

We wish to create a platform for hosting Homestays in remote areas/villages of India, particularly in regions which have high Cultural/Tourism significance, such as Rajasthan, Himachal and J&K. The homestays will be hosted by the locals of the region, through our platform. Homestays will allow locals of the remote villages to register on the platform and rent out rooms (and other services such as a tourist guide) to the tourists. This would help the tourists for experiencing the trip firsthand, with the local people helping them. Most importantly, the villagers will have a source of income, through the rent.
